About Us

With 'work from home' initiatives in full swing and not likely to dissappear anytime soon, many are finding the home office is starting to take its toll! Too many distractions, opportunities for procrastination, cramped living spaces kitted out with the office mod-cons, sub-par wifi and a distinct lack of an inpiring and motivating atmosphere.


Well, luckily, between lockdowns, there are number of great establishments out there that also call London home. From quaint little coffee shops to book-by-the-day co-working spaces, they're all raring to welcome you to their humble abode.


Whilst lockdown restrictions will inevitably change what is available to us each month, Remote London seeks to impart onto others our curated repetoire of remote-work friendly locations across the capital.


We're only a small list right now, but we're keen to hear where you have been working and help others abandon the monotony of home working! Contact us and let us know what locations you have or feedback you can share.
