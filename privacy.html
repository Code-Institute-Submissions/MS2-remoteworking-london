A word about Privacy

Whilst we use the Google API to pull data on the locations listing within this site, we have chosen to not require your location when visiting the site.


The Google Map shows the locations with central London as the default map location. It may be that future releases of this site, we choose to request your location to make listings more relevant to you, however if and when we do so, you will have the opportunity to opt out of this and any other cookie tracking.
